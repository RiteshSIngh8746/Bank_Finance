RBI Guidelines

RBI Guidelines

Understanding RBI Guidelines for Enhanced Financial Security

KYC & AML.

Know Your Customer & Anti-Money Laundering

  • Verify Customers: Banks must check identity for new and existing accounts.
  • Report Suspicious Activity: Banks have to report unusual transactions.
  • Keep Records: Proper records of transactions must be maintained.

Digital Transactions & Security

Safe and Secure Digital Transactions

Banks are required to send alerts for all card transactions to keep customers informed, with security measures in place such as PINs and OTPs to ensure safe payments. For mobile banking, banks must obtain permission from the RBI and can offer services without any daily spending limits. When it comes to online transactions, customers need to provide a mobile number, and banks are obligated to have clear rules in place for compensating customers in case of fraudulent activities.

Customer Service & Protection

Ensuring Customer Trust and Protection

  • Passbook: Available for all savings account holders.
  • Complaints Handling: Banks must have a system to address customer complaints.
